Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about AI Market services, delivery and payments.

1. How do I place an order?

Go to the AI Tools or Contact page, choose what you need and tap the WhatsApp button. Send your details, and we will reply with price confirmation, required files and delivery time.

2. What files do I need to send?

It depends on the service:

  • Video / Reels: photos, short clips, song/audio file, text to show on screen.
  • Image edits: original photo, what style you want (cartoon, clean, background change, etc.).
  • Music / BGM: type of mood (happy, sad, devotional, cinematic), approximate length.

3. How long does delivery take?

Simple reels or single photo edits may be delivered within 24â€“48 hours. Bigger or bulk orders can take longer. We will share an estimated time when you confirm your order.

4. How do I pay?

You can pay securely via Razorpay using the links on the Pricing or Donate page. Once payment is confirmed, we start or continue the work.

5. What if I need changes?

Some plans include a limited number of revisions (for example one free revision in the Pro plan). Minor adjustments like text correction, small timing changes or color tweaks are usually covered. Big changes that require redoing most of the work may be treated as a new order.

6. Which languages can you work with?

We mainly support content in English, Telugu, Tamil and Hindi. For other languages, we can discuss on WhatsApp and check if it is possible.

7. What formats will I receive?

  • Videos: MP4 (suitable for Instagram, YouTube Shorts, WhatsApp).
  • Images: JPG or PNG (high resolution where possible).
  • Audio: MP3 or WAV, depending on requirement.

8. Do you give raw project files?

Normally we deliver only the final exported file. If you specifically need raw files (like editing project files), please discuss this in advance. Additional charges may apply.

9. Can I use the content for commercial purposes?

Yes, in most cases you can use the delivered content for your own business or brand pages. However, you are responsible for ensuring that any third-party material (songs, logos, photos) you provide has proper rights.
